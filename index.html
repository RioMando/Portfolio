Bio

I fell in love with technology when I was 10 years old and a group of High School students that were passing by praised me for a battery car that I made with some pieces of wood and parts from other battery powered toys. That led me to an Electronic Engineer degree, and a Master in Informatics and Planning Systems.

Engaged about technology and the process related to teach-learn and education. Passionate about teaching robotics to kids. I envision me as a contributor of the spread of knowledge and technology, passionate to find the opportunity that allows me to do it as a professional web developer.
